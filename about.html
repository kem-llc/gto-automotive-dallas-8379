Our story

Dallas' Trusted Auto Care Since 1985

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our community safe on the road.

GTO Automotive was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: provide reliable auto repair with integrity. Over the decades, we've grown from a small shop to a trusted name in Dallas, serving generations of families. Our team brings together decades of combined experience, and we invest in the latest diagnostic technology to serve you better.

We believe in transparency and education. Every customer receives a clear explanation of their vehicle's needs, along with a fair estimate. Whether it's a minor tune-up or a major engine repair, we treat every car as if it were our own. That commitment has earned us a loyal customer base and a reputation for excellence.
